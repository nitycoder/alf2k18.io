About Us

We are delighted to inform you that Amity University Rajasthan, Jaipur is organizing it’s Annual Mega Fest- Amity Leadership Festival ( ALF ) 2018 on 16 and 17 March 2018.


Amity University Rajasthan, Jaipur has been established as “Not for Profit” education society by Dr. Ashok K. Chauhan, Founder President for imparting higher education of global standards. Amity University, Rajasthan is an exemplary institute with a multicultural spectrum of learning blended with Indian values and sanskars. We are preparing a community of students who are intellectually suited to the rapidly changing global diversity, yet retaining the Indian value system.


The university goes beyond academic excellence to Nurture Talent and organizes several academic and non-academic activities for overall development of the students. The students of the university organize technical and cultural festival every year “Amity Leadership Festival”. Amity Leadership Festival brings the students together to foster team spirit and camaraderie with their colleagues from other universities and organizations. This is a platform, where participants get opportunities to show case their talent in healthy competitive spirit. Last year, this mega event was attended by almost 20 teams from outside and witnessed by almost 4000 students of Amity & other Institutes/Universities. This academic year, the ALF will be organized on 16 th & 17 th of March 2018.
